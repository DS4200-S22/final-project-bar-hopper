Boston Bar Hopper

Zoheb Aziz, Ningyu Chen, Marco Tortolani, and Jason Zhang

Course Project as part of DS4200 S22: Information Visualization, taught by Prof. Ab Mosca, Data Visualization @ Khoury, Northeastern University.

Abstract

Our motivation behind the development of this tool is to simplify the exploration processes of all those looking to experience Boston's rich bar scene. We want you to spend time enjoying yourself at the perfect bar instead of poring endlessly through internet searches hoping to discover and acceptable establishment. To address these pain points, we have developed the Boston Bar Hopper, a custom visualization tool which leverages both classical and novel techniques to transform what was once a convoluted procedure into an enjoyable and seamless experience. This tool is suited for anyone looking to explore, discover, and identify Boston bar locations which most suit their preferences in pricing, rating, location, and much more.
